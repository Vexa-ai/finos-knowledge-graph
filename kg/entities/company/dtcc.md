Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation — US post-trade financial services
company providing clearing, settlement, and information services for equities,
fixed income, and other securities. [[FINOS]] Gold member.

## FINOS engagement: Innovate.DTCC AI Hackathon

DTCC runs an annual **Innovate.DTCC AI-Powered Hackathon** in partnership with
FINOS (and [[Broadridge]]):

- **2025 edition** (Feb 3–7, 2025, virtual): brought together finance,
  technology, and academia to build AI solutions for capital markets
  (predictive analytics, risk mitigation, automation). Teams used DTCC's AI
  Sandbox and the FINOS GitHub; winners received FINOS funding to further
  develop their solutions as open source. **Johnna Powell** (DTCC) gave the
  opening remarks. Silver Award ($75K) went to "Cyber Third Eye" — a joint
  DTCC/Standard Chartered/Codincity/DevRel Talent team building a
  cybersecurity risk-scoring platform.
- **2026 edition**: [[Matthew Bain]] ([[Morgan Stanley]], lead maintainer of
  [[Architecture as Code]]/CALM) seeded FINOS/CALM participation, opening
  finos/architecture-as-code issue #2050 with CALM-based challenge ideas across
  7 hackathon business tracks (AI-powered architecture review agents,
  CALM-driven DevSecOps pipeline generation, control-effectiveness scoring).
  Hackathon winner presentations are given at subsequent FINOS AI Readiness SIG
  meetings (e.g. issue #150) — this graph doesn't yet have an AI Readiness SIG
  entity; see `kg/EXPLORATION_LOG.md`.
