FINOS's flagship data/model platform — originated at [[Goldman Sachs]], open-sourced
via FINOS. 1,500+ stars, the most-starred FINOS project. Sub-components: legend-engine,
legend-studio, legend-pure, legend-sdlc, legend-depot, legend-shared,
legend-community-delta (plus Juju-charm operators for each). Language: mostly
Java/TypeScript.

Top legend-engine contributor: [[Rafael Bey-Hernandez]] ([[Goldman Sachs]]). Many
contributor handles carry a `-gs` suffix, reflecting Legend's origin at Goldman
Sachs before its donation to FINOS.
