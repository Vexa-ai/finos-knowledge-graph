[[FINOS]] Special Interest Group providing a space to address AI-related
challenges common to all members — onboarding, trialing, and safely adopting
(Generative) AI within financial services. 61 stars, CC-BY-4.0. In its first
year the SIG developed the [[AI Governance Framework]], which later span out
as its own standalone FINOS project. Meets bi-weekly; meeting notes recorded
as GitHub issues (e.g. issue #150, a [[Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation]]
Innovate.DTCC AI Hackathon winner presentation).

Led by three co-chairs:
- **Colin Eberhardt** — CTO, [[Scott Logic]]
- **Ian Micallef** — ICG Head of Developer Engineering, [[Citi]]
- **Madhu Coimbatore** — Head of Firmwide AI Development Platform, [[Morgan Stanley]]

Project site: https://www.finos.org/ai-readiness.
