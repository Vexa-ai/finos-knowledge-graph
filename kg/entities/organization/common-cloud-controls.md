title: FINOS Common Cloud Controls
---

Open standard (CCC) describing consistent controls for compliant public-cloud
deployments in financial services. 84 stars, Go. As a specification (rather than
OSS code) it carries a non-standard license, not the usual Apache-2.0. Related:
ccc-managed-k8s. Project site: https://ccc.finos.org.

Source: https://github.com/finos/common-cloud-controls, https://ccc.finos.org
